🚀 Rocket Surge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Rocket Surge ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Telegram Mini App game. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Telegram User ID — used to identify your account, save your progress, and display your score on the leaderboard.
  • Telegram Username and Display Name — shown on the leaderboard so other players can see your rank.
  • Game Scores and Sparks — your in-game progress stored to power the leaderboard and prize system.
  • TON Wallet Address — only if you choose to connect one via TON Connect. Used solely to send weekly prize payouts. We never initiate transactions or access your funds.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your phone number, email, or any contact information.
  • We do not access your Telegram messages or contacts.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To save your game progress (high score, gems, sparks, streak).
  • To display the public leaderboard (username and score only).
  • To send weekly TON prize payouts to winners who have connected a wallet.
  • To improve the game experience through anonymous usage analytics via Telegram Mini Apps Analytics SDK.

4. TON Connect and Wallet Data

Wallet connections are handled entirely through the official TON Connect SDK as required by Telegram's blockchain guidelines. We only receive your public TON wallet address. We never have access to your private keys, seed phrases, or funds. Disconnecting your wallet at any time removes your address from our prize payout queue.

5. Data Storage

Game data is stored in Telegram's CloudStorage and in your browser's localStorage. Leaderboard data (username, score, wallet address for winners) is stored on our secure Cloudflare Workers KV database. Wallet addresses are deleted after prizes are paid out.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Telegram — the platform this app runs on. See Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • TON Connect — for wallet connections. See TON Privacy Policy.
  • AdsGram — for optional rewarded ads. Ad providers may collect anonymous ad interaction data.
  • Cloudflare — for our backend infrastructure.

7. Children's Privacy

Rocket Surge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at @RocketSurgeGame on Telegram. We will delete your score, sparks, and wallet address from our servers within 7 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions? Contact us on Telegram: @RocketSurgeGame
